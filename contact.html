FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What time do overnight restocking deliveries occur?

Our logistical teams carry out restocking routines between 10:00 PM and 6:00 AM on weekdays. We coordinate with your building's facilities management to secure access keys and protocols, ensuring your team leaves a normal office at night and arrives to fully stocked facilities in the morning.

How are restocking inventories and volumes estimated?

During the onboarding phase, we audit your average corporate paper consumption, breakroom beverage demand, and printer ink usage. We establish a recurring baseline (such as bi-weekly restocks). Your dedicated account concierge adjusts delivery volumes depending on changes in headcount or seasonal demands.

Can we place emergency restocking orders?

Yes. Active corporate account holders have a priority telephone hotline to their account concierge. If your office experiences an unexpected surge in stationery usage or pantry demand, we coordinate same-day emergency dispatch restocks to keep your facility operational.

What geographic areas in Egypt do you service?

We currently provide full-service overnight restocking operations and custom B2B deliveries in Greater Cairo (including Smart Village, Maadi, Fifth Settlement, Giza, and Heliopolis) and Alexandria (including Smouha and Borg El Arab Industrial Zone).

Do you offer net payment credit terms for corporate clients?

Yes. For approved corporate entities, we offer standard Net-30 and Net-60 invoicing schedules. This allows procurement departments to consolidate all pantry, paper, and printing replenishment costs under a single monthly bill.

How do you ensure office building security during night restocking?

All logistics staff undergo mandatory security background checks and clearance reviews. We establish supervised access protocols with your facility manager. Furthermore, crews sign comprehensive NDAs before entering offices or boardroom premises.
